We value your privacy

In the digital world we live in, privacy online is more important than ever! That's why we strive to collect as little information about you as we can.

What we collect

When you use our Garmin apps, you provide us with some information, namely your heart rate, your username, and nickname. This information is necessary to provide the basic functionality of our apps.

What happens to your data

Data from our apps is processed quickly and deleted within a few hours. We do not store any of your data, and no data is ever shared to advertisers.
